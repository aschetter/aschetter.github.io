About Me

Professional


After earning a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration from Bowling Green State University, I fueled my passion for service by partnering with two different international organizations. First, I served one year as a project coordinator for Camp Adventure Youth Services offering enrichment activities for children of deployed and active-duty parents living on military bases around the world. Then, I dedicated three years to the Peace Corps building and improving programs for at-risk children and youth in the Republic of Moldova.


Recently, I relocated to Austin to pursue a career change and explore the ever-changing challenges presented by the tech industry. This summer, I attended MakerSquare: an immersive software engineering and web design program, where I began my journey of developing and perfecting my agile web development skills.


Personal


I enjoy learning something new every day through travel, exploring new cultures, writing, and photography. Before I visit a new country, I study its history, politics, and culture to gain a better understanding of its people and appreciate what makes it unique. Then, I like to document my experiences in the country by writing down funny stories or observations and taking pictures of interesting scenes.


In addition, I'm an avid sports fan, primarily playing and watching basketball, soccer, and football. Finally, popcorn is one of my passions. I like to make a batch of old-fashioned popcorn and share it with friends and family.
